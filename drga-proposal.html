A Modern Digital Ecosystem with a Purpose.

DigiSoal proposes to create a complete digital ecosystem for DRGA (Dr Gwen & Associates). This includes a primary website for DRGA and a distinct, purpose-built website for the Dr. Gwen Ramokgopa Foundation. Our approach focuses on delivering a seamless and professional digital presence tailored to the needs of the organization.

A Professional & Dynamic Visual Identity

Our design philosophy combines a clean, corporate aesthetic with a dynamic, vibrant palette to instill confidence and convey forward-thinking leadership across all digital platforms.

Proposed Colour Palette

Deep Navy Blue

A foundational color conveying professionalism, stability, and trust.

Vibrant Teal

A dynamic accent color symbolizing innovation and growth.

Soft Grey

Used for supportive backgrounds and clean, spacious layouts.

Charcoal Black

Ensures high contrast and readability for all text.

Proposed Typography

Heading: Inter Bold

Body Text: Inter Regular. The modern, clean lines of Inter provide professional readability on all devices, making it a perfect choice for a corporate presence.
